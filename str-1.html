   Street no.77 project

Execution of main waste water line within street no. 77 district no. 768, using micro-tunneling and the execution of the pumping station. One of the strategic projects in Iraq.

The project consists of a line to transport waste water, 6 Km long, with 3 Km long concrete pipe of 1400mm ID, and 3 Km long of a 1600 mm ID.

We are excuting using a German machine guided by laser, and leading technics to control the soil from the ground.

Also the construction of a pumping station with a total discharge of 4.25 m3/sec. in district 768.

The project will service Al Nasr, Maamil, Bawiya, Al Batol, and Al rashard Districts within Districts ( 771, 772, 774, 775 and 781). Waste water will be transported from the residential areas through the pumping station newly constructed and discharge the waste water through the line connecting to Al Khansaa lines that goes through street 77, than discharged to the treatment plants.
