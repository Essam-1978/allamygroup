   North East Line

Main waste water lines that connect the north east line with the main pumping station related to it. One of the strategic projects in Iraq.

Waste water transportation lines, starts from the north bank of Baghdad to the main north east transportation line. It was executed using open trench method, with depths between 4 to 7m depth. It consists of two main lines:

The first is 4 Km long concrete pipes of 1600, 1800, and 2000 ID and serve all Kamaliya and Ubaidi districts and what is around those.

The second is 3.5 Km long with concrete pipes of 1200, and 1400 mm ID. It also includes the pumping main lines and downstream lines connected to the pumping station. It serves Al Suhafieen, district 757, Rahmah, Safir and Shamaiyah district and what is around that.

In addition to the pumping station which pumps waste water to the main north east line (Al Khansaa). The pumping station includes three aueger pumps.

The project will be connected in the future with Al Khansaa line as planned.

The project was handed over to the end-user officially.

In addition to the above items we constructed rain water network along street no. 77, to the intersection of Al Oboor, and we poured concrete along the line and paved with asphalt with side curbstone and median strip.
