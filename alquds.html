   Al Quds project

Project to execute the main line, to finish Al Quds line, from 83 circle to Al nift club, using Micro-tunneling, within Al Sadr 2. One of the strategic projects in Iraq.
Concrete pipes for waste water are laid underground with ID of 2000 mm and a total length of 3 Km.
Project is executed using micro-tunneling, ie laying the pipes underground under and existing street 5 m deep, without digging an open cut in the ground. Our micro-tunneling machines are made in Germany by MTS.
The line is from 83 circle to Al Nift Club intersection within Shaab, to serve districts (315-327-323-321-319-317-325) in Shaab, Our, Trabiya, Binok and Sumer districts.
In the project we are using expat staff with previous experience, and we work 27/7.
