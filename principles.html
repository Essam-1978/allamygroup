  Core Principles

The core principles and goals of the company are based on successfully evaluating its activities, and among these goals:

1. The company guarantees the quality of its projects and that it would be executed according to the engineering specifications, and within the contract time. 

2. Projects are executed using modern methods, maintaining the safety of the staff inside the site, in addition to educating engineering and working staff, with safety guidelines, through periodic seminars and safety instructions on site.

3. Develop capabilities of staff and competencies by participating in courses and scientific seminars, as well as participating in Exhibitions and conferences.

4. The use of the best engineering practice and standard procedures in the projects through using high quality materials within the specifications. The company aspires to contribute to the progress of rebuilding of Iraq.

5. Our companies are among the companies that keep pace with technical developments that contribute greatly to the application of modern methods in building , construction methods, excavation and laying of concrete pipes in projects for sewage networks for waste water and construction of pumping stations related to that, and small projects associated with those networks.
