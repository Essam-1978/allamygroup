  Construction Machinary

The strength of any construction company, comes in part from the heavy equipment it owns, making the company capable of executing projects using its own resources without the need for the services of other companies to lease the needed equipment to finish big construction projects.
Because our management believes in the above, we added so many construction equipment and vehicles to our fleet, thus making our company capable of executing any project within its field.
In addition we own a mobile workshop that was made especially for us in Germany, to maintain the vehicles and heavy construction equipment, in addition to the workshops that are based in our headquarters.
